New ultra-low-noise microphones. Using advanced computational audio to remove more noise around you than ever, AirPods Pro 3 let you zone in (or out) when you need to. Voice Isolation. AirPods Pro 3 reduce background noise and isolate voices during calls. So whether you're on the bus or in a café, every conversation is loud and clear.

Adaptive Audio. AirPods Pro 3 combine Active Noise Cancellation with next-level Transparency, which makes your voice and the world around you sound even more natural and vibrant, intelligently adapting to your environment to prioritize what you need to hear, when you need to hear it.

Audio performance

The sound of science.

Powered by the Apple-designed H2 chip, AirPods Pro 3 feature a completely redesigned acoustic architecture that brings breathtaking, all-around-you audio to everything you listen to — from your pump-up playlist to your weekly FaceTime call with family.

A new multiport acoustic architecture with more precise airflow control — featuring an inward-facing microphone that points directly into the ear canal — delivers deeper bass, vivid vocal clarity, and a wider soundstage, so you never miss a note.

A custom-built driver and amplifier work with the H2 chip to create high-definition, three‑dimensional sound, with superlow distortion during playback.
